ABOUT US

GOLDEN KING' LECHON is a dish consisting of roast suckling pig. It is commonly used in the Spanish cuisine of Castile, with the variants of native and large white being the most popular ones, although also popular in CEBU and in some places in the regions of Zamboanga city and dipolog city. This oven dish is traditionally prepared in an earthenware pot and served hot with a crispy crust. It is recommended to combine cochinillo asado with wine.
